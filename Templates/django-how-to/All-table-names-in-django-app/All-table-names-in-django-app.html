Method 1

First of all we have to import a django module apps form django.apps. Then we have to call get_app_config(app_name).models.items() . This wiil give a dictionary containing model_name & model_class.

All database's operations can be performed over model_class

from django.apps import apps
all_models = apps.get_app_config('your_app_name').models.items()
for model_name,model_class in all_models:
    print(model_name)
